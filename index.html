Zhaohui Xia is an Associate Professor and PhD Supervisor at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), where he serves as the head of the CAD Research Center, Principal Investigator of the National Key R&D Program (Young Scientist Track) and Huazhong Distinguished Scholar.
He received his Ph.D. in Mechanical Manufacturing and Automation from HUST in 2016, followed by postdoctoral research in medical-engineering integration at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (USA) and Harvard Medical School/BIDMC (2016–2020).

Research interests: Computational Design for Additive Manufacturing, Geometric Modeling, Topology Optimization, Isogeometric Analysis, and Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning.

I am actively seeking creative and highly motivated MS and PhD students who are passionate about research.

夏兆辉，华中科技大学机械科学与工程学院副教授，博士生导师，国家重点研发计划青年项目首席科学家，湖北省高层次海外人才，华中卓越学者。 现担任华中科技大学国家企业信息化应用支撑软件工程技术研究中心（武汉）负责人。

主要从事机械产品数字化设计及其工业软件方向的教学与科研工作，主讲《机械设计理论与方法（三）》等课程3门，围绕航空航天、武器等重点领域关键产品设计需求，从事自主可控CAD/CAE设计分析优化一体化软件技术等研究。
