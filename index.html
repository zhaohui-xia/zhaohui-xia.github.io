Zhaohui Xia is an Associate Professor and PhD Supervisor at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), where he serves as the head of the CAD Research Center, Principal Investigator of the National Key R&D Program (Young Scientist Track) and Huazhong Distinguished Scholar.
He received his Ph.D. in Mechanical Manufacturing and Automation from HUST in 2016, followed by postdoctoral research in medical-engineering integration at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (USA) and Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center (BIDMC) of Harvard Medical School (2016–2020).

I am actively seeking creative and highly motivated MS and PhD students who are passionate about research.
